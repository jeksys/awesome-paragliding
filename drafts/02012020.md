## 5 HAZARDOUS ATTITUDES in flying
1. Anti-authority: “Don’t tell me!”
2. Impulsivity: “Do something quickly!”
3. Invulnerability: “It won’t happen to me!”
4. Macho: “I can do it!”
5. Resignation: “What’s the use?”
